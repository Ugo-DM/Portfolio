Who Am I ?

Hello there! Since you've been scrolling this much to access this content, I'd first like to thank you! You've already seen my projects, but you still don't know who I am? Don't worry, you'll soon know it all!

My name is Ugo. I'm 25 and I'm a Developer for only a few years. Actually, before October 2020 I've never really written a single line of code, never used a Linux terminal, and never centered a div :o !!

Since I was a kid, I've always wanted to create things & express myself with style. I've done it by practicing ballet from 3 to 12, then with Music by playing guitar, bass, or cello during a period of 10 years from 13 to 23. I still play guitar sometimes but only for my cat & dog (and maybe my neighbors).

But after 4 years of trying to graduate from university while struggling with part-time jobs, I couldn't see where I was going with my life.
Then came 2020, a rough year for everyone, a year of questioning about our lifestyles & what we wanted for this world. As a growing young adult, I wanted change! Staying home, playing video games & going shopping for the elderly in my neighborhood, I started watching videos about how video games were made. Quickly I also found videos about apps & websites and started wanting to create some of my own...

Not knowing where to begin or even how to learn such a power, I looked around the internet to find resources and bits of advice. As you guessed there was a lot of all that. But I found a school that proposed a 6-month remote course for wannabe developers, O'clock. I applied, got accepted, and started in October 2020.

I've learned a lot about development & how to manage projects during these 6 months! Then I passed the exam at the end of 2021. Knowing that all I'd learned wasn't enough for me to be a better web dev, I applied to Wild Code School as an internship student for a year.

I'll start my internship in March 2023. I'll keep you updated here and also on my socials which you can find about on the next & final section of this website if you agree to scroll just a little bit more...
