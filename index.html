Who Am I ?

Hello there ! Since you've been scrolling this much to access this content, i'd firstly like to thank you ! You've already seen my projects, but you still don't know who I am ? Don't worry, you'll soon know it all !

My name is Ugo. I'm 25 and I'm a Developer only since few years. Actually, before October 2020 I've never really wrote a single line of code, never used a Linux terminal and never centered a div :o !!

Since I was a kid, i've always wanted to create things & express myself with style. I've done it by practicing Classical Dancing from my 3 to 12, then with Music by playing guitar, bass or cello during a period of 10 years from my 13 to 23. I still play guitar sometimes but only for my cat & dog (and maybe my neighboors).

But after 4 years trying to graduate at university while strugling with part-time jobs, I couldn't see were I was going with my life.
Then came 2020, a rough year for everyone, a year of questionning about our livestyles & what we wanted for this world. As a growing young adult, I wanted change ! Staying home, playing video-games & going shopping for elderly in my neighborhood, I started watching videos about how videogames were made. Quickly I also found videos about apps & website, and started wanting to create some of my own...

Not knowing were to begin or even how to learn such a power, I looked around the internet to find ressources and advices. As you guessed there were a lot of all that. But I found a school who proposed a 6 months remote course for wannabe developpers, O'clock. I've applied, got accepted and started in October 2020.

I've learned a lot about development & how to manage projects during these 6 months ! Then I passed the exam at the end of 2021. Knowing that all I've learned wasn't enough for me to be a better web dev, I've applied to Wild Code School as an internship student for a year.

I'll start my intership in March 2023. I'll keep you updated here and also on my socials wich you can found about on the next & final section of this website, if you agree to scroll just a little bit more...
